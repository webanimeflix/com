Episode 1: Mission Start

An elite spy named Twilight is tasked with infiltrating an important political event. To maintain his cover, he adopts a daughter, Anya, who is a telepath, and marries a woman, Yor, who is secretly an assassin.

Episode 2: Our Beginning

Twilight and Yor begin their fake marriage and Anya starts attending school. Anya, with her telepathic abilities, starts reading people's minds but struggles to fit in.

Episode 3: Prepare for the Interview

Twilight prepares Anya for her entrance exam at Eden Academy, where he must ensure that she performs well enough to secure his mission's success.

Episode 4: The Prestigious School

Anya gets accepted into Eden Academy, but trouble arises when Twilight has to navigate the world of prestigious school politics to protect his mission.

Episode 5: Will They Be Able to Meet the Mission?

Twilight tries to bond with Anya, and the family faces challenges while hiding their true identities. Yor gets involved in a dangerous situation during a mission.

Episode 6: Disarming the Enemy

Twilight's mission intensifies as he faces a dangerous adversary while Yor has her own complicated assignments to manage.

Episode 7: A Secret Crisis

Anya faces a crisis at school and Twilight has to handle a new dangerous mission that puts the family at risk.

Episode 8: Beneath the Peaceful Facade

Twilight digs deeper into his mission while juggling his role as a father and husband. He gets involved in high-stakes espionage.

Episode 9: The Moment of Truth

The family is pushed to their limits as Twilight and Yor face more threats while trying to keep their secret lives hidden from Anya.

Episode 10: The Unexpected

Twilight and Yor get entangled in unexpected events that threaten their carefully constructed lives. Anya tries to support her parents.
