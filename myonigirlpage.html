My Oni Girl

"My Oni Girl" is a Japanese anime film about a shy high school boy named Hiiragi who unexpectedly encounters Tsumugi, an Oni (demon) girl searching for her mother in the human world, leading them on a magical summer adventure to the Oni spirit realm, where he learns to overcome his insecurities and embrace his own strength while navigating the complexities of their unique bond.
