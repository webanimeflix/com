Denji is recruited by Makima into Public Safety. He starts adapting to his new life as a devil hunter under her guidance.

Denji partners with Power, a fiend, to rescue her cat Meowy. However, they face the Bat Devil, and Denji unleashes his chainsaw powers.

Episode 4: Rescue

Denji battles the Bat Devil and saves Power and Meowy. Power begins to respect Denji.

Episode 5: Gun Devil

Makima explains the devastating power of the Gun Devil. Denji, Aki, and the squad begin hunting its fragments.

Episode 6: Kill Denji

Denji and his team enter the Eternity Devil's realm. The devil traps them and demands Denji's life.

Episode 7: Taste of a Kiss

Denji sacrifices himself to fight the Eternity Devil. After victory, he receives an unexpected kiss from Himeno.

Episode 8: Gunfire

Makima and other Public Safety members are ambushed by Gun Devil's associates, leading to massive casualties.

Episode 9: From Kyoto

Makima survives the ambush, revealing her mysterious powers. Denji faces Samurai Sword in a brutal fight.

Episode 10: Bruised & Battered

Aki struggles with loss, and Denji trains under Kishibe to grow stronger. Makima manipulates events behind the scenes.

Episode 11: Mission Start

The squad prepares to raid Samurai Sword's hideout. Denji and Power show their improved abilities.

Episode 12: Katana vs. Chainsaw

Denji battles Samurai Sword in a high-stakes showdown, ultimately defeating him. The season ends with new questions about Makima's intentions.
